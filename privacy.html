Privacy Policy for Fishing Tracker

Last Updated: June 20, 2026

Fishing Tracker ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Saved Locations: You may save names, latitudes, and longitudes for your favorite fishing coordinates. This data is stored locally on your device.
  • Support Communication: If you contact us via email, we may retain your email and correspondence details to help resolve your issue.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Location Information: With your permission, our app accesses your device's GPS coordinates (latitude and longitude) to fetch localized tide, weather, and wind data. This information is processed in real time and is not stored on our servers.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous usage statistics (e.g., features opened, coordinates searched) may be collected to help improve our service.
  • Device Info: Anonymous details such as device model, operating system version, and app version for diagnostics and crash reporting.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver real-time and predicted marine weather forecasts (tides, waves, wind).
  • Allow you to search for coordinates and save custom marine locations.
  • Cache meteorological data locally for offline viewing when out of network range.
  • Troubleshoot bugs, resolve application crashes, and optimize performance.
  • Comply with legal requirements.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal data. We only share information with third-party service providers as necessary to run the app:

  • Weather and Marine APIs: Our app queries external services (such as Open-Meteo and TideTurtle) to retrieve live weather data. The query includes the latitude and longitude of your selected location. This data is processed anonymously by these providers under their respective privacy policies.
  • Google Play Services: For crash reports and developer metrics.

4. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

4.1 For EEA Users (GDPR)

If you reside in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Access & Portability: Request details on any personal data we hold.
  • Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): Since all coordinates, preferences, and cached forecasts are stored locally, you can erase all data immediately by clearing the app cache/data in your Android settings or uninstalling the app.
  • Object / Restrict: Revoke location permission at any time through your device settings.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: dev@fishingtracker.app

4.2 For California Users (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected, used, and shared.
  • Request deletion of your personal information (which can be completed locally by clearing app data or uninstalling).
  • Opt out of any sale of personal data (we do not sell your personal data).
  • Receive non-discriminatory service for exercising your CCPA rights.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Our app is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If a parent or guardian believes that a child under 13 has shared any contact details with us, please contact us at dev@fishingtracker.app and we will delete it immediately.

6. Data Retention

All coordinate locations, units selection, and weather caches are saved locally on your device storage. We do not maintain user accounts or store personal data on remote servers. Uninstalling the app permanently purges all stored locations and caches from your device.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable physical and electronic measures to secure your data. All network calls to external APIs use secure HTTPS transmission to prevent interception.

8. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with:

  • Open-Meteo API: For fetching weather, wind, waves, and geocoding.
  • TideTurtle API: For tide forecasts.

Please refer to their respective terms and privacy policies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted here with the new "Last Updated" date.

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us at:
